Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

There is nothing as ubiquitous as the words "chocolate chip cookies."

This recipe will guarantee a dozen chocolate chip cookies with the ingredients listed below.

Use the same sized cup and teaspoon for measurement accuracy.

Guaranteed to be everything you expected and more, but not less!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose white flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup coarsely chopped walnuts

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a medium bowl, cream together the butter, white sugar and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the egg, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Combine the flour and baking soda, stir into the creamed mixture.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips and walnuts.
  6. Drop by rounded ice cream scoops onto a cookie sheet, and press down slightly to flatten; cookies should be about 2 inches apart.
  7. Bake for 11 to 14 minutes in the preheated oven.
  8. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
  9. Enjoy!
